About us

We are a group of GreenValley students with goal to publish
a fantasy book for all children so that we can spread the joy of stories.

We hope you can help us on the way by reading our book. Though it took a lot of time and hard work we are happy to tell you that we have achieved our goal.
We hope and know you will enjoy this book.
